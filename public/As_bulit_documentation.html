What we do

As-Built Documentation

Aprosol Company Nigeria Limited prepares detailed as-built drawings that accurately capture the final state of facilities after construction, installation, or modification. These drawings document all changes made during the project, ensuring that clients have a precise and up-to-date record of their infrastructure. This critical service supports future maintenance, operations, and any subsequent upgrades, providing clear, reliable information for safe and efficient facility management.
