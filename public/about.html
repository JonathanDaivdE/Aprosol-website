About Us

Absolute Project Solution (Aprosol) Company Nigeria Limited, first registered in 2003 and later started operation in 2010, is a trusted provider of comprehensive Engineering, Procurement, Project Management, Manpower Supply, Installation & Construction (EPC), and Commissioning services to the Oil & Gas industry and industrial infrastructure clients across Nigeria.

With extensive experience spanning downstream, upstream, offshore, and onshore projects, we have successfully executed numerous projects nationwide, demonstrating our commitment to delivering world-class solutions that drive operational excellence and sustainable growth for our clients.

Through our partnership with Combined Engineering & Integrated Solutions (CEIS)—a SMEC company based in Pakistan with solid oil & gas expertise, we strengthen our capabilities in engineering design, project management, and commissioning.

We specialize in instrumentation and control, electrical power engineering design, construction/installation, while also providing comprehensive Engineering services in process, civil/structural piping, pipeline. With our ability to quickly deploy skilled project teams anywhere in the region, we ensure every project starts strong and delivers exceptional results.
